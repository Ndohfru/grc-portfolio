# Third-Party / Vendor Risk Assessment

## Objective
This project demonstrates practical third-party risk evaluation, vendor oversight thinking, and documentation of vendor-related control and compliance concerns in a Governance, Risk, and Compliance context.

## Business Scenario
This artifact is structured as a sample vendor risk assessment for a business relying on third-party service providers, where security, compliance, operational dependency, and data handling risks must be evaluated before or during engagement.

## What This Demonstrates
- Third-party risk identification
- Vendor due diligence thinking
- Inherent risk awareness
- Control and compliance review
- Risk documentation
- Governance support

- Vendor risk assessment spreadsheet
- PDF export
- Preview screenshot
