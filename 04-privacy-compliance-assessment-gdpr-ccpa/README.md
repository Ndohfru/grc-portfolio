# Privacy Compliance Assessment (GDPR / CCPA)

## Objective
This project demonstrates practical privacy compliance analysis against GDPR and CCPA requirements, with focus on identifying privacy obligations, comparing regulatory expectations, and evaluating data governance and compliance readiness.

## Business Scenario
This artifact is structured as a sample privacy compliance assessment for a business that collects, stores, and processes personal information, where privacy obligations, consumer rights, data handling practices, and regulatory exposure must be evaluated.

## What This Demonstrates
- Privacy requirement interpretation
- GDPR and CCPA comparison
- Compliance gap identification
- Data governance awareness
- Documentation discipline
- Governance support

- Privacy compliance assessment spreadsheet
