# Enterprise Risk Register

## Objective
This project demonstrates practical risk identification, scoring, ownership assignment, control awareness, and treatment planning in a Governance, Risk, and Compliance context.

## Business Scenario
This artifact is structured as a sample enterprise risk register for a business operating in a regulated environment, where operational, compliance, technology, and third-party risks require structured tracking and oversight.

## What This Demonstrates
- Risk identification
- Likelihood and impact scoring
- Inherent and residual risk thinking
- Control awareness
- Risk ownership
- Treatment planning
- Documentation discipline

## Files Included
- Enterprise risk register spreadsheet
- PDF export
- Preview screenshot

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Risk Management
- Compliance Monitoring
- Control Assessment
- Documentation
- Operational Risk
- Governance Support
